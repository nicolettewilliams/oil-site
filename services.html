Services

Exco Crude Oil specializes in purchasing and transporting most variations of crude oil from heavy, low API to lighter, higher API condensates in varying qualities (BSW) including off-spec products throughout Texas and the Republic of Mexico. Through our affiliations and strategic relationships and access to over 40 units, Exco Crude Oil can transport crude oil from almost any location.

Through its affiliations and strategic relationships, Exco Crude Oil can seamlessly import most products through virtually any Port of Entry into the United States either land or water borne. Our team of specialists has extensive experience dealing with HazMat imports and customs requirements on both sides of the border.

Exco Crude Oil, through its affiliates, currently counts on 3 assets for crude oil blending in South, West and East Texas. Based on our varying locations, we have the ability to blend most variations of crude oil quality to industry specifications from anywhere in Texas or the Republic of Mexico.

Exco Crude Oil offers competitive pricing based on volume, quality, location and frequent payment terms while also excelling in customer service and creating long-term relationships. Exco Crude Oil pioneered next-day payment terms with remission of product invoices the next business day. Additionally, with sufficient storage facilities, there is virtually no limit to production volume.
