James L. Jensen attended Brigham Young University and has a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Utah. He is the current president and owner of Maxim Crude Oil, LLC, a full-service crude oil logistics, treatment and oil marketing business. Mr. Jensen has been actively engaged in various aspects of the oil and gas business for over 40 years. As founder of Jenex Petroleum, Inc., in the early 80s the company grew to become listed as one of the 100 fastest growing private companies in America. During the mid-90s, Jenex acquired the assets of several public and private oil and gas companies including those of National Oil Company and its affiliate partnerships which had raised in excess of $300 million dollars in oil and gas drilling funds. Over the last 15 years, Mr. Jensen’s companies have drilled and completed in excess of 90 producing wells in Colorado, Texas and Oklahoma. Mr. Jensen sold his oil and gas production company in 2012 but maintained his ownership in Maxim Crude Oil which currently operates four crude oil treatment plant facilities in the states of Texas and New Mexico. Currently Maxim maintains crude oil purchasing relationships with major refiners such as Exxon, Sunoco and Phillips 66. To date Mr. Jensen’s combined treatment facilities have purchased and processed in excess of 40 million barrels of crude oil. In addition to crude oil operations, Mr. Jensen is active in numerous charitable and social organizations and is an active member of his church, married and the father of four boys.
